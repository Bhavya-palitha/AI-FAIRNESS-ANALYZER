The Problem Statement

AI-based decision systems used in hiring, finance, and healthcare often exhibit bias against certain groups such as gender or socio-economic categories. This leads to unfair and unethical outcomes. There is a critical need for a system that can detect and reduce bias in AI models to ensure fairness and transparency for all.

Hiring

Qualified candidates from certain backgrounds are unfairly rejected due to biased training data.

Finance

Loan approval systems discriminate based on socio-economic status or zip code.

Healthcare

Diagnostic tools show lower accuracy for minority groups.

Our Solution

AI Fairness Analyzer is an intelligent platform that helps organizations identify and eliminate bias in their AI systems.

It scans datasets and model predictions, calculates fairness metrics, applies correction techniques, and provides clear visual proof of improvement.

Our tool makes ethical AI practical and accessible for real-world applications.

How It Works - 4 Simple Steps

01

Upload Dataset

Upload your CSV file or connect directly to your AI model pipeline. Supports hiring data, loan records, medical datasets, etc.

02

Bias Detection

Our system automatically analyzes the data using 8+ fairness metrics including Demographic Parity, Equal Opportunity, and Disparate Impact.

03

Automatic Correction

Choose from multiple correction methods like reweighing, adversarial debiasing, or post-processing to reduce bias.

04

View Results

Get beautiful interactive visualizations, fairness score, and downloadable PDF report.
